Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, located in the Himalayas, bordered by China to the north and India to the east, south, and west. It's known for Mount Everest, the world's highest peak, and for being the birthplace of Buddha. The capital and largest city is Kathmandu. Nepal is a multi-ethnic, multi-lingual, and multi-religious nation with Nepali as its official language. It transitioned to a federal democratic republic in 2008, replacing the monarchy.
